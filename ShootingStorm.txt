Shooting Storm

Objective:

This game objective is survive and destroy a enemy and get high score. This game can challenge another people by play and see other people score. 

Control:

This game will comtrol by using a W, A, S, D to move a actor and use a spacebar to fire and use R to fire rocket.

Difficulty will depend on player score. If player score is high it will more difficulty and has a lot of enemy.
